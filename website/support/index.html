Frequently asked questions

The FAQ answers the common ones. A few in short:

  • Do I need an Apple Watch? No — everything works phone-only. A Watch adds sleep stages, overnight HRV and per-minute heart rate, which sharpen the scores.
  • Do I need to pay for AI? No. All scoring, logging, prayer times and planning are deterministic and local. Apple Intelligence and a local Ollama model are no-cost AI options.
  • Why does a ring say "calibrating"? Sleep and Readiness compare you to your own baseline, which needs about a week of data.
  • My prayer times look off. Check calculation method, branch and madhab in Settings → Faith — Asr in particular differs by madhab — and confirm the app has location access.
  • My widget isn't updating. Widgets read a snapshot the app writes; open the app once and give iOS a moment. iOS also throttles widget refreshes system-wide.

Report a bug or request a feature

File it on GitHub Issues. The most useful reports include your iOS version, iPhone model, what you did, what you expected, and what happened instead. Please don't paste API keys or personal health details into a public issue.

Email

For anything else, including privacy questions and App Store issues: suhails.letterbox@gmail.com. It's a one-developer project, so replies can take a few days.

Your data

Win the Day stores your day on your device and has no account system, so there is nothing for support to look up on your behalf and no password to reset. See the privacy policy for exactly what leaves the phone.
