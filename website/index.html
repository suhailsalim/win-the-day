Win the Day

Run your whole day — scored honestly, coached by AI, stored only on your phone.

Private by design

No backend, no account, no analytics. Your data is JSON on your phone and AI keys live in the iOS Keychain. The only network calls are to the AI provider you choose and free public data services for weather and food lookups.

Deterministic scores, not vibes

Sleep, Readiness, Active, Eating and Prayer-timing scores are pure on-device formulas over your Apple Health data and your logs — the same inputs always produce the same number. The AI explains your scores; it never invents them.

Faith as a first-class pillar

Astronomical prayer times computed on-device and madhab-aware, on-time prayer tracking with neutral timing bands, a Qibla compass and fasting windows — all optional, and all customizable.
