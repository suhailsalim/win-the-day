# Widgets & Apple Watch

## Home Screen & Lock Screen widgets

Add widgets from the iOS widget gallery:

- **Non-negotiables ring** — your day's habit completion at a glance.
- **Prayer widgets** — next prayer and time, with Lock Screen (accessory) variants.
- **Fasting** — elapsed/remaining fast time.
- Water, score, and week-progress surfaces on the larger sizes.

Widgets read a small snapshot the app publishes — they update shortly after you log something in
the app. If a widget looks stale, opening the app refreshes it.

## Live Activities

Real-time Lock Screen / Dynamic Island activities for:

- the **prayer window** after each adhan,
- **study sessions** (running timer with controls),
- **focus sessions** (depleting ring with pause/stop).

## Apple Watch

The watch app mirrors your day — score, prayers, water, fasting — and supports quick actions
(logging water, marking prayers) that sync back to the phone. **Complications** put your score,
next prayer, or fast timer on the watch face.

!!! note "Install quirks"
    Watch installs over Wi-Fi can be flaky. If the watch app fails to install, install it from the
    **Watch app on the iPhone** instead.
