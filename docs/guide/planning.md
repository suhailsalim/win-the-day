# Planning your week

The **Plan** tab is where days become a week.

## Week outlook

An AI-written look-ahead built from your recent stats, insights, upcoming sessions and occasions,
and — with Calendar access — your **real calendar events**. Beside it, a Mon–Sun grid shows each
day as won / logged / today at a glance.

## Routine → sessions

Define your recurring weekly template as **routine blocks** (weekday, time, kind, duration —
workout, study, personal training, and more). The app materializes them into concrete, dated
**sessions** you can check off, reschedule, or skip. Session reminders arrive as notifications, and
sessions can sync to Apple Calendar.

## Occasions & trips

Birthdays, anniversaries, events, and **travel** — each with a date (annual recurrence supported),
person, and location:

- **Plan it with AI**: get ideas, a practical checklist, and (for trips) a day-by-day itinerary.
  Paste a booking confirmation and the plan builds around your actual flights and hotel.
- Add your own **context** — budget, who's coming, must-dos — and the AI honors it.
- **Refine**: tell the AI what to change ("add a beach day", "cheaper options") and it revises the
  existing plan instead of starting over.
- Checklist items and itinerary stops are fully editable by hand, and occasions can sync to your
  calendar with lead-time reminders.

## Weekly review

A guided end-of-week review summarizes wins, misses, and trends, and rolls what you learned into
next week's outlook — a scheduled notification keeps the habit.
