# Today & rings

The **Today** tab is the cockpit: a ring row up top, then a stack of modules you can reorder and
toggle (Settings → Today layout, or the controls on section headers).

## The ring row

Thick, Apple-style rings pinned to the top of Today — show anywhere from **3 to 6** (up to 4 sit
on one row; 5 or 6 wrap onto a second row so no ring shrinks). Built-in ring sources:

| Ring | What fills it |
|---|---|
| **Sleep** | Last night's sleep quality (duration vs. your need, efficiency, consistency, stages) |
| **Readiness** | Recovery vs. your own baseline — HRV-led, with resting HR, respiration and sleep |
| **Active** | Today's activity strain from active calories (Watch sharpens it, but isn't required) |
| **Eating** | How today's nutrition tracks your targets (calories, protein, macros, timing…) |
| **Prayer** | On-time prayer performance across the five daily prayers |

You can also create **custom rings** over your own metrics — hydration goal, study/focus hours,
protein target, steps target, calorie budget, non-negotiables done, or Qur'an pages — each with a
title, goal, and color.

- **Tap a ring** to expand it: the exact score, the factors that moved it, and an
  **Explain this ring** button that has the AI walk you through it in plain language.
- **Edit rings** (add, remove, reorder, 3–6 visible) in the ring editor — Settings → Rings, or the
  sliders button on the ring row.
- A **grey ring with "—"** means the data genuinely isn't available (e.g. no sleep recorded) —
  the app never shows a fake zero.
- New scores show **"calibrating (n/7)"** until a week of baseline data exists.

## Non-negotiables & the day score

Your daily checklist — the app calls them **non-negotiables** — is fully configurable via the
**Edit** button on its section. Each one belongs to a pillar (health / spirituality / work /
custom) and is either:

- **Manual** — tap to complete, or
- **Auto-linked** — satisfied automatically from your data: protein target hit, prayers done,
  steps, active energy, water, study hours, or sleep.

The **day score** counts satisfied non-negotiables; complete at least ~60% and the day is
**won** — that feeds your streak and the week grid. Special day statuses (sick, rest, travel) let a legitimate
off-day protect your streak.

## Quick logging

Everything loggable is one or two taps from Today: water (bottle view with per-glass tapping),
weight (auto-filled from Apple Health when available), steps and active energy (auto-filled),
workouts (auto-detected from Apple Fitness with heart-rate zones, plus manual entries), study and
jog minutes, and the five meal boxes.

## Weather & tips

A compact weather tile (Open-Meteo, no account) sits beside a rotating strip of short, context-aware
tips generated a few times a day — with sensible built-in tips whenever AI is off or offline. A
static tip card appears at the top of the page only when this rotator isn't on screen (weather
module off, no weather data, or browsing a past day), so you never get two advice cards stacked up.

## Browsing other days

The header's date controls (and the History screen) let you view and edit any past day — useful for
backfilling last night's dinner or yesterday's prayers. Editing a past day recomputes that day's
scores only.
