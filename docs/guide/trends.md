# Trends, history & reports

## Trends

Charts over your history: weight (shown as a smoothed trend, never noisy dailies), steps, calories
and protein, scores, prayers, study hours, and more. The weekly eating card adds energy balance and
a projected weight change with honesty flags (aggressive-deficit warnings, sodium/water-weight
caveats after salty days).

## History

Browse or jump to any past day and edit it in place — meals, prayers, habits, notes. Past days keep
their score factors, so you can always answer "why did that day score like that?"

## Progress photos

Attach photos to any day; the photo timeline puts any two side-by-side for before/after comparison.
Photos stay in the app's local storage on your phone.

## Imports

- **InBody / body-composition reports**: photo, PDF, or pasted text → AI parses it → saved to your
  records, mirrored to weight, and written to Apple Health.
- **Lab reports**: parsed the same way; supported values (lipids, HbA1c, vitamins…) go to Apple
  Health, and results join your health profile for the coach.

## PDF report

Generate a shareable PDF summary of your data — useful for a doctor's visit or a personal review —
via the system share sheet.
